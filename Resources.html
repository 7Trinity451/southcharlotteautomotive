What is the average cost of an oil change?

An oil change averages $35-$75 for conventional oil and $65-125 for full synthetic. You should get a conventional oil change every 3 months or 3,000 miles. If you are getting a synthetic oil change, it should be done every 5 months or 5,000 miles.

What is the average tune up cost?

The average tune-up cost can range from $100-$800+. Tune-up services should be done every 60,000-100,000 miles.

What is the average brake job cost?

The average brake job cost can range from $300-$600 per axle(front or rear) for pads and rotors.

What is the average cost for a suspension shock or strut replacement?

The average cost for a suspension shock or strut replacement is between $1,057 and $1,268.

What is the average cost for a transmission replacement?

The replacement cost for a mainstream car with an automatic transmission can range from $2,500 to $ 5,000.

What is the average cost for an engine replacement?

On average, drivers can expect to pay between $5,000-$10,000 for an engine replacement.
